Places of India






Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in 1632.

It symbolizes eternal love and is one of the most iconic monuments in the world.






India Gate

India Gate in New Delhi is a 42-meter-tall war memorial built in honor of 70,000 Indian soldiers.

It stands as a symbol of sacrifice and is beautifully illuminated every evening.






Charminar

Charminar is a historic monument and mosque in Hyderabad, known for its four stunning minarets.

Built in 1591, it serves as a central landmark and a vibrant cultural hub.






Hampi

Hampi, located in Karnataka, is the ruins of the once-glorious Vijayanagara Empire.

Its majestic stone temples and boulders attract historians and travelers alike.






Gateway of India

The Gateway of India in Mumbai was built during the British era to welcome King George V in 1911.

It overlooks the Arabian Sea and is a major tourist destination and historic symbol.
